Tourism

Parking is very limited, so take public transportation or a taxi instead! There are many things to do on this bridge. Explore the bridge by taking a hike, enjoying the wonderful views from atop the bridge. You can take a guided tour, where a professional takes you across the bridge, showing you the best views and telling you the story of the bridge. No matter who you are, the Golden Gate Bridge always haas an experience for everyone
